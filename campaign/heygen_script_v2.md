# BOSS Copy — HeyGen Video Script (Version 2)
### Built using the BOSS Copy process: Hook → Problem → Story → Proof → CTA
**Runtime:** Approx. 2 minutes 30 seconds
**Tone:** Real. Direct. Like a business owner talking to another business owner over coffee.
**Delivery note:** Conversational pace. Pause after the hook. Let the story breathe. Pick up energy at the proof section. Warm and confident on the close — not salesy.

---

## THE HOOK
*[Direct to camera. No intro. No "hey guys". Start mid-thought.]*

"You know what the most expensive thing in your business is right now?

It's not your tools. It's not your van. It's not even your wages.

It's the customers you're not getting — because your marketing copy is invisible.

And I'm going to show you exactly how to fix that in about two minutes."

---

## THE PROBLEM
*[Stay on camera. Lean slightly forward. This should feel like a confession, not a pitch.]*

"Here's what I kept seeing.

Brilliant tradespeople. Brilliant business owners. People who are genuinely the best at what they do — and their Facebook page says 'We offer quality workmanship at competitive prices.'

Nobody reads that. Nobody calls because of that. That copy is costing you jobs every single week and you don't even know it.

And it's not your fault. You didn't start your business to become a copywriter. You started it because you're good at the work.

But in 2026, if your words don't work — your business doesn't grow. Simple as that."

---

## THE STORY — HOW BOSS COPY WAS BUILT
*[Sit back slightly. This is the pivot. Tell it like a story, not a feature list.]*

"So here's what happened.

We run a system called the BOSS AI Agent. It's an AI infrastructure system — we help businesses set it up so that AI handles the repetitive, time-consuming stuff while the owner focuses on what actually matters.

And one day we decided to use our own system to build something.

Not to prove a point. Not as a demo. We genuinely wanted to solve the copy problem — for trade businesses, for small business owners, for anyone who knows they need better marketing but doesn't have the time or the skill to write it.

So we used the BOSS AI Agent system — the exact same system we sell — and we built BOSS Copy.

AI built an AI copy tool.

And here's the part that matters: it works. Not because it's clever. Because we built the entire copywriting process into it. The hooks. The structure. The psychology of what makes someone stop scrolling and pick up the phone. That's not just AI generating words — that's a system that writes the way a professional copywriter thinks."

---

## THE PROOF — SHOW IT WORKING
*[Pick up energy here. This is the moment. Deliver these examples with conviction.]*

"Let me show you what I mean.

A plumber enters his details into BOSS Copy. Forty-seven seconds later, here's what comes out:

*'Burst pipe at midnight? We answer. We show up. We fix it — and we don't charge you double because it's after hours. Call now and we'll be there in 60 minutes or less. Guaranteed.'*

An electrician. Thirty-eight seconds:

*'Your switchboard is older than your kids. That's a problem. Old switchboards overheat, trip constantly, and in the worst cases — they start fires. Book a safety check this week. Forty-five minutes. One hundred and forty-nine dollars. You'll know exactly where you stand.'*

A landscaper. Forty-two seconds:

*'Your neighbours are already talking about their lawn. Make sure they're talking about yours. We transform overgrown, embarrassing outdoor spaces into the yard you've been meaning to sort out for three years. Free quote this week — jobs booked through April.'*

*[Pause. Let it land.]*

That's not a template. That's not a chatbot you have to argue with for twenty minutes. That's a purpose-built system that understands trade businesses, understands what customers respond to, and writes copy that actually does the job.

Facebook ads. Google ads. Emails. Social posts. Website headlines. SMS campaigns. Eight formats. Under sixty seconds each."

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Runtime estimate:** 2 minutes 20 seconds – 2 minutes 45 seconds depending on pace.

**The three copy examples are the heart of this video.** Deliver them slowly and clearly. Let the viewer actually read them on screen as you say them. Consider adding the text as an on-screen overlay for each one — white text on dark background, same style as Post 5 image.

**B-roll suggestions:**
- The three copy examples appearing on screen as text overlays (full screen, dark background, orange accent — match the brand)
- Screen recording of BOSS Copy generating copy in real time
- Trade business footage — tools, worksite, tradesman on phone — for the problem section
- Hua Hin / event imagery for the CTA section

**Lower thirds / on-screen text:**
- "BOSS Copy — bosscopy.kabooyaaassistant.ai" (persistent)
- "Built using the BOSS AI Agent System" (during the story section)
- Each copy example as a full-screen text card — plumber, electrician, landscaper
- "KABOOYAA 3-Day AI Build With You Workshop — Hua Hin, April 2026" (during event mention)
- End card: URL + logo

**Pacing:**
- Hook: fast, punchy, no pauses
- Problem: slower, empathetic — let it feel real
- Story: conversational, mid-pace — this is the credibility section
- Proof: energetic — the examples should feel exciting, not read like a list
- CTA: warm and direct — confident, not desperate
