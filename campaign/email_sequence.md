## SEQUENCE NOTES

**Timing:** Send Email 1 immediately. Email 2 on Day 3. Email 3 on Day 7. If someone clicks the BOSS Copy link in Email 1 or 2, consider triggering a separate nurture sequence focused on the product rather than the event.

**Segmentation suggestion:** Anyone who replies "Hua Hin" should be moved to an event-specific follow-up sequence with dates, agenda, and booking details.

**Subject line A/B test options:**
- Email 1 alt: "I used AI to build an AI tool. Here's what happened."
- Email 2 alt: "You're going to want to see this before April."
- Email 3 alt: "The offer closes when the event fills up."

**Tone note:** These emails are written to sound personal and direct — like a message from a real person, not a broadcast. Keep formatting minimal. No heavy HTML templates. Plain text or very light styling performs better for this type of warm list email.
