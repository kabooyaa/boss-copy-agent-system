FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do I need any copywriting experience?
None. You fill in 5 fields describing your business, customer, and offer. The engine does the rest. If you can describe your business in plain English, you can use BOSS Copy.
What AI is powering this?
BOSS Copy runs on your own Manus account. You connect your API key during setup — so you're using your own AI credits, not ours. You're in full control of your usage and costs.
Does it work with High Level?
Yes. Email and SMS outputs use High Level's native merge fields ({{contact.first_name}}, {{contact.company_name}}, {{contact.phone}}, etc.) so you can paste directly into your HL workflows, automations, and campaigns.
Can I use it for any industry?
Yes. The BOSS formula works for any business that sells anything to anyone. Tradies, agencies, coaches, e-commerce, SaaS, local businesses — the formula adapts to your specific customer and offer.
What if I already have copy I want to improve?
Two ways. Use the "Improve My Copy" tab to paste in any existing copy — BOSS rewrites it using the formula and shows you exactly what changed and why. Or, if you're on Platinum, use the Website Copy Analyser — paste your URL and BOSS scrapes your entire site, identifies every weak headline and vague claim, and delivers before/after BOSS formula rewrites for every section.
What if I'm not happy with the copy it generates?
Use the Feedback & Rewrite loop — it's built into every result. Type exactly what you want changed ("make the headline more urgent," "add a specific number," "change the tone to be more direct") and BOSS rewrites that section instantly. You can iterate as many rounds as you need. There's no limit.
What does the Full Website Copy Generator do?
It's a Platinum feature that builds copy for your entire website in one session. Choose "Build From Scratch" — fill in your business details, select the pages you need (Home, About, Services, Contact, FAQ, and more), and BOSS generates complete BOSS-formula copy for every page: headlines, body copy, bullet points, CTAs, and SEO meta descriptions. Or choose "Analyse & Improve" to paste your existing site URL and get a full rewrite. Each page has its own Feedback & Rewrite panel so you can refine any section.
What does the Competitor Copy Analyser do?
Paste a competitor's URL. BOSS scrapes their site, identifies their copy strengths and weaknesses, and builds a full Battle Plan — a section-by-section breakdown of how to beat them with BOSS-formula copy that directly counters their positioning. It's a Platinum-only feature.
Is there an API for my AI agent?
Yes. BOSS Copy has an optional external API endpoint. If you're using an AI agent like Open CLAW, you can connect it via bearer token authentication so your agent generates BOSS-quality copy on demand — without the user ever needing to open the app.
