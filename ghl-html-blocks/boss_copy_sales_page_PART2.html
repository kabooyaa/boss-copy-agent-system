FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do I need any copywriting experience?
None. You fill in 5 fields describing your business, customer, and offer. The engine does the rest. If you can describe your business in plain English, you can use BOSS Copy.
What AI is powering this?
BOSS Copy runs on your own Manus account. You connect your API key during setup — so you're using your own AI credits, not ours. You're in full control of your usage and costs.
Does it work with High Level?
Yes. Email and SMS outputs use High Level's native merge fields ({{contact.first_name}}, {{contact.company_name}}, {{contact.phone}}, etc.) so you can paste directly into your HL workflows, automations, and campaigns.
Can I use it for any industry?
Yes. The BOSS formula works for any business that sells anything to anyone. Tradies, agencies, coaches, e-commerce, SaaS, local businesses — the formula adapts to your specific customer and offer.
What if I already have copy I want to improve?
Two ways. Use the "Improve My Copy" tab to paste in existing copy and get a full BOSS-formula rewrite with a change log. Or use the Feedback & Rewrite loop on any generated result — type exactly what you want changed and BOSS rewrites it instantly. Iterate as many rounds as you need.
Can BOSS Copy write copy for my entire website?
Yes — on Platinum. The Full Website Copy Generator has two modes: paste your existing site URL and get a full BOSS-formula rewrite of every section, or choose "Build From Scratch" and fill in your business details to generate complete copy for every page — Home, About, Services, Contact, FAQ, and more. Every page includes headlines, body copy, bullet points, CTAs, and SEO meta descriptions. Export the whole package as a text file.
Can I use it to analyse a competitor's website?
Yes — on Platinum. The Competitor Copy Analyser lets you paste any competitor's URL. BOSS scrapes their site, identifies their copy strengths and weaknesses, and builds a full Battle Plan — a section-by-section breakdown of how to beat them with BOSS-formula copy that directly counters their positioning.
Is there an API for my AI agent?
Yes. BOSS Copy has an optional external API endpoint. If you're using an AI agent like Open CLAW, you can connect it via bearer token authentication so your agent generates BOSS-quality copy on demand — without the user ever needing to open the app.
What is the S.T.A.R.T. framework and why does it matter for social posts?
S.T.A.R.T. is Austin Armstrong's proven viral content framework: Stop scroll (pattern-interrupt hook), Talk about the problem (agitate the pain), Align with the audience (show you understand them), Resolve the problem (deliver real value), Tell them what to do (CTA). The Viral Post Generator builds every post using this exact structure, so you're not guessing at what makes content go viral.
Why does the Viral Post Generator put the link in the first comment instead of the post caption?
Facebook and LinkedIn algorithms actively suppress posts that contain links in the caption — they want to keep users on-platform. The "First Comment CTA" strategy (popularised by Austin Armstrong) puts the link in the very first comment you post immediately after publishing. This avoids the algorithm penalty while still directing your audience to your link. BOSS Copy generates both the post body and the ready-to-paste first comment so you can execute this strategy in seconds.
